DataDir - path to save and load location
ModelDir - path to directory of model files, looks for:
	handIcon.obj  handIcon.mtl
	carIcon.obj  car2Icon.mtl
	planeIcon.obj  planeIcon.mtl
	bicycleIcon.obj  bicycleIcon.mtl
	magnifyingIcon.obj  magnifyingIcon.mtl
	birdIcon.obj  birdIcon.mtl
	fileIcon.obj  fileIcon.mtl

GridSize - a positive integer for grid snapping, 1 for off
OrientToViewer - on or off, controls rotation of shapes to face viewer
